Inventions by Nikola Tesla

  • Tesla Coil : The Tesla coil is a transformer used to produre low-current, high-voltage, high-frequency alternating current.
  • Tesla Turbine : The Tesla turbine was an turbine style engine that used combustion to make the disks rotate.
  • Tesla Valve : A Tesla valve is a fixed-geomety passive check valve. It allows fluids to flow prefrentially in one direction without any moving parts.
  • Magnifying Transmitter : The magnifying transmitter is used to create standing waves of electricity that travel through air and light up flourescent bulbs at a distance up to 1km.
  • Niagra Falls Transformer House : Tesla's system for alternating current worked well and have become the standard for hydroelectrical power.
  • Radio : Tesla used his coils to transmit and receive radio waves up to 50 miles.
  • Induction Motor : The first commutato-free 3 phase induction motor. This type is motor is still used in vacuums, power tools and other appliances.
  • Neon Lamp : Althouh he did not invent neon lights be improved them vastly allowing them to make unique designs and form words.
  • Alternating Current : While he is not the inventor of AC power he made it easy to be used.
  • Radio Controlled Boat Tesla invented the Teleautomaton. A small toy boat remote controlled using radio waves.
  • Shadowgraph : Another name for X-rays. Although it was initially developed by someone else; Tesla made improvements to them to render clearer images.
